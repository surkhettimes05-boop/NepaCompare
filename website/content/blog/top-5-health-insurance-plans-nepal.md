Health insurance is no longer a luxury in Nepal—it's a necessity. With medical inflation rising every year, having a robust health insurance policy protects your family's savings from unexpected hospital bills.

In this guide, we break down the top health insurance plans available in the Nepali market for 2026.

## 1. Comprehensive Family Floater Plans

Family floater plans cover your entire family under a single sum assured. If you have a spouse and children, this is significantly cheaper than buying individual policies. Most plans now cover:
*   In-patient hospitalization
*   Pre and post-hospitalization expenses
*   Daycare treatments

## 2. OPD (Outpatient Department) Coverage

Historically, Nepali insurance companies only covered admitted patients. However, newer plans now include OPD limits, allowing you to claim doctor consultation fees and pharmacy bills. 

## 3. Critical Illness Riders

When comparing plans, always look for a Critical Illness rider. This provides a lump sum cash payout upon diagnosis of severe conditions (like cancer or kidney failure), ensuring you have the liquid cash needed for immediate treatment.
